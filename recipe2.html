Classic Margherita Pizza

⏱ Prep Time: 45 mins 🔥 Difficulty: Medium

Description: A traditional Italian favorite with a thin crust, tangy tomato sauce, and fresh mozzarella. It's a classic that never disappoints.

Ingredients

  • 1 portion of Pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup Tomato sauce
  • 1 cup Fresh mozzarella, sliced or torn
  • Handful of Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat the Oven: Preheat your oven to its highest setting (around 250°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  2. Shape the Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface until it forms a thin circle.
  3. Sauce it up: Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Add Cheese: Distribute the fresh mozzarella evenly over the sauce.
  5. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  6. Garnish & Serve: Remove from the oven, drizzle with olive oil, and garnish with fresh basil leaves before slicing.
