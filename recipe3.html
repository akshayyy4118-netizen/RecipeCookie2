Restaurant-Style Paneer Tikka Masala

⏱ Prep Time: 40 mins 🔥 Difficulty: Medium

Description: Grilled cubes of paneer cooked in a deeply flavorful, spiced onion and tomato base. The ultimate vegetarian comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer (Cottage Cheese), cubed
  • 1 Capsicum (Bell pepper) and 1 Onion, cut into large square pieces
  • 1/2 cup Thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Gram flour (Besan)
  • 2 Tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 large Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder & 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 2 tbsp Cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Mix yogurt, gram flour, a pinch of turmeric, salt, and 1 tsp oil. Coat the paneer, capsicum, and large onion pieces in this marinade. Rest for 15 mins.
  2. Heat a pan and lightly grill the marinated paneer and veggies until they have light brown spots. Remove and set aside.
  3. In a kadhai (wok), heat oil and sauté the finely chopped onions until soft. Add coriander powder and turmeric.
  4. Add the tomato puree and cook on medium heat until the raw smell disappears and the masala thickens.
  5. Add half a cup of water to adjust consistency, bring to a boil, then gently drop in the grilled paneer and veggies.
  6. Simmer for 5 minutes, garnish with fresh coriander leaves, and serve with roti or paratha.
