Easy 3-Ingredient Chocolate Fudge

⏱ Prep Time: 10 mins (+ 2 hrs Chilling) 🔥 Difficulty: Easy

Description: Forget complicated candy-making techniques! This foolproof recipe uses sweetened condensed milk to create incredibly dense, rich, and smooth chocolate fudge with zero stress.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Semi-sweet chocolate chips (high quality)
  • 1 can (14 oz) Sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup Unsalted butter, cut into small cubes
  • 1 tsp Pure vanilla extract
  • A pinch of flaky sea salt (optional, for topping)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prepare the Pan: Line an 8x8 inch square baking pan with parchment paper. Leave a little bit of paper hanging over the edges so you can easily lift the fudge out later.
  2. Combine Ingredients: In a medium-sized, heavy-bottomed saucepan, combine the chocolate chips, sweetened condensed milk, and the cubed butter.
  3. Melt Gently: Place the saucepan over low heat. Stir continuously and gently using a silicone spatula. Keep stirring until the chocolate chips and butter are completely melted and the mixture is smooth and glossy. (Do not let the mixture boil or get too hot, as the chocolate can seize!)
  4. Add Flavor: Remove the pan from the heat immediately. Stir in the vanilla extract until fully incorporated.
  5. Set in the Pan: Pour the warm fudge mixture into your prepared baking pan. Use your spatula to spread it out evenly and smooth the top. If using, sprinkle a pinch of flaky sea salt over the top now before it sets.
  6. Chill: Place the pan in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until the fudge is completely firm.
  7. Slice and Serve: Once firm, use the overhanging parchment paper to lift the block of fudge out of the pan. Place it on a cutting board and use a sharp knife to cut it into small, rich squares. Serve cold or at room temperature.
