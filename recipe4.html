Creamy Fettuccine Alfredo

⏱ Prep Time: 20 mins 🔥 Difficulty: Easy

Description: You don't need a restaurant to enjoy a luxurious, silky pasta dish. This authentic Alfredo sauce uses just a few high-quality ingredients to create a rich, comforting meal in under 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 400g Fettuccine pasta (or your favorite shape)
  • 1/2 cup Unsalted butter (1 stick)
  • 1 1/2 cups Heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups Freshly grated Parmesan cheese (avoid pre-shredded)
  • 1/4 tsp Salt, plus extra for pasta water
  • 1/4 tsp Freshly cracked black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil the Pasta: Bring a large pot of heavily salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta water before draining.
  2. Start the Sauce: While the pasta is boiling, melt the butter in a large skillet or pan over medium-low heat.
  3. Add Garlic & Cream: Add the minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant (do not let it brown). Whisk in the heavy cream. Let the mixture come to a gentle simmer and cook for about 3-4 minutes until it slightly thickens.
  4. Melt the Cheese: Turn the heat down to low. Gradually whisk in the freshly grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is completely smooth. Season with salt and cracked black pepper.
  5. Toss the Pasta: Add the drained fettuccine directly into the sauce. Toss gently using tongs to ensure every noodle is thoroughly coated. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up.
  6. Serve: Serve immediately in warm bowls. Top with a little extra grated Parmesan, cracked black pepper, and chopped fresh parsley.
